(1) Automatic collection, processing, and use of personal data

(1.1) Web service log files

When you access the web pages of our website with your browser, our server automatically stores the information required for the operation of a web service in log files, including the name of your browser, the name of your Internet service provider, the IP address of your PC, the address of the web page from which you accessed our website, the name of your operating system, the web pages you visit on our website, as well as the date and duration of your visit. This information is temporarily stored in the log files and automatically deleted afterward. The purpose of this storage is, in case of a problem, to enable an analysis of activities which possibly caused the fault.

(1.2) Cookies

Our online shop uses cookies, which are small files transferred to your PC by the web server. These files are used to track your movement from page to page in our online shop or to manage a shopping cart.

On the one hand, cookies are differentiated according to the duration of their storage. So-called session cookies are automatically deleted when you close your browser. A session cookie can be used to manage a shopping cart or to record products of special interest, whereas permanent cookies remain stored on your PC. Information about the storage duration is provided by your browser. A permanent cookie can be reread the next time you visit our website. To prevent this, you can delete the permanent cookie after your visit to our website. Permanent cookies can for example be used to provide information from a shop website for use by an online payment service.

On the other hand, cookies are differentiated according to their origin. So-called first-party cookies always originate from the website indicated in your browser's address bar. So-called third-party cookies originate from websites which you have not visited directly, but have been tied into in the first-party's website by means of images or advertisements. Your browser can tell you the sources of the cookies stored on your PC.

Most browsers are set to accept cookies. To disable this function, please change the corresponding settings of your browser. If your browser does not accept any cookies, you might not be able to visit all the web pages of our online shop without any trouble. To place an order, it is necessary for you to set your browser to accept session cookies.

(1.2.1) How can I disable cookies?

You can easily adjust your browser settings to disable all cookies. Please note: If you completely disable your cookies, you will not be able to use all the functions of our online shop.

(1.2.1.1) Blocking first-party cookies

To block first-party cookies in the most frequently used browsers, just click one of the links below:

  • For Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer
  • For Internet Explorer 7 and 8: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-GB/windows-vista/Block-or-allow-cookies
  • For Internet Explorer 9: http://windows.microsoft.com/en-GB/windows7/How-to-manage-cookies-in-Internet-Explorer-9
  • For Google Chrome: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647?hl=en&hlrm=en
  • For Safari on iPhone, iPad or iPod Touch: https://support.apple.com/en-gb/HT201265

If you use a different browser or a different end device, check your browser's help pages to find out how to block first-party cookies.

(1.2.2) Cookie management in the UrbanDesi online shop

In accordance with European cookie law (ePrivacy Directive), the placement of permanent cookies that serve any purpose other than securing correct communication with the web server is subject to the user's consent.

Assuming that your browser is set to accept cookies, we store permanent cookies for your language selection (udesi-country and country cookies) and your language preference (udesi-lang and lang cookies) on your PC. We store the selected currency in an udeis-currency cookie.

The shopping cart and your wish list are managed in session cookies, which are called PHPSESSID, session-1, session-2, session-3, and shop.

Opt-out options are provided, see the following section, for the placement of a cookie used during the analysis of site use by Google Analytics (see 1.3 below). These cookies are called or begin with _ga or _gat.

When you visit the UrbanDesi online shop, a banner shows you that the online shop uses cookies. Via this banner you can access information about our use of cookies, consent to their use, or close the banner. If you do not use an opt-out option, we will interpret your conduct as meaning that you accept the placement of the cookie as described here, including cookies used during the analysis of site use by Google.

The e-mail newsletter is sent via a service provider, which collects data regarding readers' usage on our account. This data allows us to assess the efficacy of advertising campaigns and to continuously improve our offer.
